I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at MGH/Harvard Medical School, hosted by Prof. Juan Eugenio Iglesias and Prof. Bruce Fischl.

I obtained my PhD degree from the Department of Computer Science at Stony Brook University, advised by Prof. Chao Chen. During my PhD, I have also been fortunate to spend time at United Imaging Intelligence (UII) and Allen Institute for Cell Science to explore various biomedical scenarios/applications.

Before that, I obtained my master and bachelor degrees from Tsinghua University and Huazhong University of Science and Technology, respectively.

Research Interests: My research interest is Medical AI, and I am focusing on developing core AI/ML algorithms applied to medical imaging problems. In particular, I am interested in:

  1. Topology-Driven Deep Image Analysis [NeurIPS'19, ICLR'21, MICCAI'21, ICLR'22, ECCV'22, NeurIPS'22, ICLR'23b, NeurIPS'23, ECCV'24a, MICCAI'24Wa]
  2. Uncertainty Estimation and Its Applications [ICLR'23a, ICLR'23b, ICCV'23b, NeurIPS'23]
  3. Learning with Imperfect Data [ICLR'23a, ICCV'23a, ICCV'23b, ECCV'24a, MedIA'24]
  4. Biomedical Applications [ECCV'22, ICCV'23a, ECCV'24b, MICCAI'24Wa, MICCAI'24Wb, MedIA'24, MICCAI'24a, MICCAI'24b, MICCAI'24c]
