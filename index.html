I pioneer Topology-Driven Deep Image Analysis. Please feel free to contribute to the GitHub repo!

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at MGH/Harvard Medical School, hosted by Prof. Juan Eugenio Iglesias and Prof. Bruce Fischl.

I obtained my PhD degree from the Department of Computer Science at Stony Brook University. Before that, I obtained my master and bachelor degrees from Tsinghua University and Huazhong University of Science and Technology, respectively. I was selected as a 2023 Catacosinos Fellow.

Research Interests: My research focuses on Machine Learning for Healthcare, with an emphasis on developing core AI/ML algorithms for healthcare applications. In particular, I am interested in:

  1. Topology-Driven Deep Image Analysis: TopoLoss (NeurIPS'19), DMT (ICLR'21), TopoTrigger (ICLR'22), TopoInteraction (ECCV'22), Warping (NeurIPS'22), TopoSemiSeg (ECCV'24)
  2. Trustworthy Machine Learning: Consistency Ranking (ICLR'23), Structural Uncertainty (ICLR'23, NeurIPS'23), , Calibrating Count (ICCV'23), Hierarchical Uncertainty Estimation (ICLR'25)
  3. Multimodal AI and Generative AI (GenAI): Brain-ID (ECCV'24), RankByGene (arXiv'24), TopoCellGen (CVPR'25), Learn2Synth (ICCV'25)
  4. Healthcare Applications: Modality-Agnostic Tumor Segmentation (ICCV'23), Anomaly-Guided Lesion Segmentation (MedIA'24), Scalable MRI Segmentation (arXiv'25)
